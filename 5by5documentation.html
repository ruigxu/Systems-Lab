5 in 5 REDO

In an effort to improve my productivity levels and focus more on consistently making things rather than making only perfect things, this challenge had me make one thing per day with a few restrictions, which meant to prevent "what do i do?!" feeling and encourage spitting out work.

My restrictions were:

  • Use acrylic paint
  • 2D
  • No bigger than 9inchesx12inches

    • Unintended themes ended up being the color palette - I got excited about my new paints - and subject - when in doubt, go back to plants, as I've always done.

    TAKEAWAYS

  • - when trying to do something new, in this case, scheduling creative work, know yourself and don't try to experiment too far or else you'll psyche yourself out
  • - small measurements require small brushes
  • - blue and purple are not eyesores like you thought they were 5 years ago
  • - self consciousness is totally useless; have no shame regarding your own style
